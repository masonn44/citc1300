Art Style

The art style blends soft, natural color palettes with intricate background designs that evoke a sense of nostalgia and timelessness, reflecting the passage of years in the story. The art style emphasizes the weight of memory and the emotional subtleties of a world where even great heroes must eventually come to terms with their mortality.
